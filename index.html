FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer?

We offer comprehensive courses in Robotics, AI, IoT, and Drone technology. Our programs are designed for students of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced learners.

How long are the courses?

Our courses range from 3 months to 1 year, depending on the program and complexity. We also offer short-term workshops and bootcamps for specific skills.

Do you provide lab setup services?

Yes, we provide complete lab setup services for schools and institutions, including equipment installation, training, and ongoing support.

What age groups do you cater to?

Our programs are designed for students aged 8-18 years, with age-appropriate content and difficulty levels for each group.

Are there any prerequisites?

No prerequisites are required for our beginner courses. We start from the basics and gradually build up to advanced concepts.
